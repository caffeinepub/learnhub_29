Our Story

Founded in 2020, LearnHub started with a simple mission: make quality education accessible to everyone, everywhere.

We believe that the right course at the right time can change someone's life. That's why we partner with world-class instructors to bring you up-to-date, practical courses that help you build real skills.

Today, LearnHub serves over 50,000 learners across 120 countries with 200+ courses across technology, design, and business.
